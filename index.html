About

I build things that move, sense and think. I'm a mechanical engineer working at the intersection of robotics, automation and intelligent manufacturing, where mechanical design meets control, sensing and autonomy.

I led a 30-person team building a programmable Mars rover and secured funding to start a LiDAR navigation robot. I've written an RRT path planner for a nonholonomic robot, modelled ECU-based ABS control in Simulink, and turned solid-mechanics theory into a validated MATLAB tool that scored 100%. I care about designs that survive contact with reality, so I build, test, refine, and document what breaks.
