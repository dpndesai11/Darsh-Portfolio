Who Am I?

I am a second-year Mechanical Engineering student at the University of Portsmouth with an interest in full-cycle system design, spanning concept development, modelling, design for manufacture, and system integration.

I have worked on multiple projects involving each stage of the manufacturing process, including material selection, design validation, prototyping, testing, and production preparation. This has developed my ability to translate theoretical designs into physical systems through iterative improvement under practical constraints.
